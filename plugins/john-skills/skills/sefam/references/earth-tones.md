# Style: Earth Tones

**Feel:** Warm linen + amber editorial. Soft, grown-up, unhurried — a magazine spread.

**Best for:** a warm, human-scale explainer that still reads as mature and considered.

**Build prompt:**
- Background: linen / cream (`#f3ede3`). Text: warm dark brown (`#3a2f28`).
- Palette: warm browns and amber (`#b5763a`), with a muted sage accent (`#8a9a7b`).
- Serif or humanist-sans body: `Georgia, serif` or `"Segoe UI", system-ui, sans-serif`.
- Editorial feel — generous leading, comfortable margins, a clear type hierarchy. Section heads in amber or brown.
- Soft, minimal treatment: thin rules, maybe one restrained pull-quote. No hard shadows or loud borders.
- Body text ~1.1rem, ~60–70ch line length.
- Light-only design.
