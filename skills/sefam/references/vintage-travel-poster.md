# Style: Vintage Travel Poster

**Feel:** WPA-era flat lithography — confident poster hierarchy, rich muted color, strong headings.

**Best for:** announcements and exec summaries that want a bold, optimistic, well-composed feel.

**Build prompt:**
- Flat blocky shapes, no gradients pretending to be photos. Layered flat color regions.
- Limited muted-but-rich palette: mustard (`#d9a441`), teal (`#2e6e6a`), brick (`#b5442e`), cream (`#f2e8d5`).
- Strong condensed display headings: `"Oswald", "Arial Narrow", system-ui, sans-serif` (use a condensed system fallback — no webfonts).
- Subtle paper grain via a faint SVG/CSS noise overlay.
- Confident hierarchy — one big headline, clear section titles, generous spacing. Poster, not brochure.
- Body text in a plain readable sans/serif (~1.1rem), on cream.
- Light-only design.
