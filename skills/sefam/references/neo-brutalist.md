# Style: Neo-brutalist

**Feel:** Brutalist bones but playful and nostalgic — thick borders and hard shadows with saturated blocky color.

**Best for:** an internal update with personality; a change you want to feel bold and fun without being childish.

**Build prompt:**
- Thick black borders (`border: 3px solid #000`) on cards and blocks.
- Hard offset drop-shadows, no blur: `box-shadow: 6px 6px 0 #000`.
- Saturated blocky fills behind sections — electric blue (`#2d5bff`), hot pink (`#ff4da6`), yellow (`#ffd400`). Flat, no gradients.
- Chunky sans headings: `system-ui, "Helvetica Neue", sans-serif`, heavy weight.
- Rounded corners are OK but keep them modest; the shadows and borders carry the look.
- Body text on a light panel for readability (~1.1rem).
- Light-only design.
